# North Star

Build the best Hospitality Operating System.

OPSCORE must first become stable at Vincent Resort Hotel.

Only after Vincent proves the platform is stable should OPSCORE expand to multiple companies and eventually become a SaaS platform.

Everything we build must move us closer to this destination.

OPSCORE is the business platform.

OKE is the Engineering Operating System.

OPSCORE runs the business.

OKE understands, documents, audits, protects, and continuously improves OPSCORE.

Together they create a system that can grow without losing engineering quality.

# Decision Compass

When multiple solutions exist...

Choose the solution that:

• Preserves architecture

• Eliminates hardcoding

• Keeps responsibilities clear

• Improves maintainability

• Reduces future technical debt

• Makes future onboarding easier

---

# Engineering Principles

Audit before coding.

Understand before modifying.

Document before expanding.

Stabilize before scaling.

Finish before replacing.

Simple before clever.

Consistency before speed.

Knowledge before assumptions.

---

# What We Do NOT Build

We do not build features because they are interesting.

We build features because Vincent Resort needs them.

We do not redesign stable systems.

We do not add unnecessary complexity.

We do not compromise architecture for short-term convenience.
