# OKE Engineering Constitution

Version: 1.0  
Status: Foundation Standard

---

## Purpose

This constitution defines the permanent engineering rules of OKE.

OKE must remain understandable, extensible, and maintainable even as new specialists, commands, workflows, and intelligence layers are added.

---

## Core Law

Understand first.  
Analyze second.  
Change last.

No implementation should happen before the system impact is understood.
