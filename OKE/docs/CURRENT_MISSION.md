# CURRENT MISSION

Version: 1.1

Status: ACTIVE

Last Updated: 2026-06-28

---

# Primary Mission

Stabilize Vincent Resort Hotel.

OPSCORE must become a trusted production system for Vincent Resort before expanding to other companies or SaaS.

OKE must help rebuild and audit OPSCORE from the foundation upward.

---

# Core Rule

Everything must be reviewed.

Nothing is assumed stable until audited.

OKE must check every module, every workflow, every engine, every report, and every integration before it becomes part of the production standard.

---

# Current Execution Order

Priority 1

Foundation Audit

Objective

Review the whole OPSCORE architecture from the start and define the standard that every module must follow.

---

Priority 2

Finance

Objective

Build a reliable financial foundation that the operations team can trust.

---

Priority 3

Cash Management

Objective

Achieve reliable cash flow tracking, approvals, remittances, turnovers, reconciliation, and reporting.

---

Priority 4

Approval Engine

Objective

Guarantee that every approval produces one consistent and auditable business outcome.

---

Priority 5

POS

Objective

Create a setup-driven POS with zero hardcoded operational data.

---

Priority 6

Reservation System

Objective

Build the OPSCORE reservation system because Cloudbeds is already disconnected and Vincent Resort needs an internal reservation foundation after POS.

---

Priority 7

Inventory

Objective

Build inventory around reusable engineering standards established by POS and operations.

---

Priority 8

Payroll

Objective

Audit, stabilize, and preserve payroll reliability without unnecessary redesign.

---

Priority 9

Workforce

Objective

Audit and stabilize attendance, scheduling, employee portal, and workforce operations.

---

# Deferred Objectives

The following are postponed until Vincent Resort is stable:

- Multi-company rollout
- SaaS platform
- Marketplace
- Public APIs
- Advanced AI automation
- External company onboarding

---

# Definition of Success

The current mission is complete when:

- Vincent Resort runs daily operations on OPSCORE.
- Finance is trusted.
- Cash Management is trusted.
- Approval outcomes are reliable.
- POS is production-ready.
- Reservation System is usable internally.
- Inventory is production-ready.
- Payroll is trusted.
- Reports are trusted.
- Users can operate without constant manual correction.
- OPSCORE has a clear engineering standard enforced by OKE.
